Experience

  • My research interests lie in computer vision and machine learning, with particular interests in salient object detection, semantic segmentation and weakly-supervised learning.
  • 2014.09 - 2019.07, Ph.D., Department of Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University, under the supervision of Prof. Huimin Ma.
  • 2018.01 - 2018.07, visiting Vision and Learning Lab at University of California, Merced, under the supervision of Prof. Ming-Hsuan Yang.
  • 2010.09 - 2014.07, B.Eng, Department of Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University.
